# Privacy Policy

SoundCloud Account Manager stores SoundCloud session data (cookies and localStorage) locally in your browser using the extension's built-in storage. This data is used solely to switch between saved SoundCloud accounts.

**No data is collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party.** All account data remains on your device and is never sent to any external server.

The extension only accesses soundcloud.com to read and restore session data for the purpose of account switching.

You can delete all saved account data at any time by removing accounts from the SoundCloud profile dropdown or by uninstalling the extension.

If you have questions, open an issue at https://github.com/xxwif/soundcloud-account-manager/issues.
